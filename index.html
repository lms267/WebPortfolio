Taking Care of Yourself in the Service Industry: Your Guests are Important, But So Are You!

"Self-compassion is simply giving the same kindness to ourselves that we would give to others." - Christopher Germer

Hi, my name is Lily Sullivan. I am 22 years old and have been working in the industry since I was 16. Every server I have ever talked to have all said something to the tune of "I'm tired", "My body always hurts," "I feel like I never have time for myself and I'm always working," etc. I have always flirted with the idea of a blog of this nature, and this is my attempt to remind all of my food service workers out there: It is okay to take time for yourself! You spend your entire shifts catering to other people and making sure that all of their needs are met, but when do you take time to think about your own needs? This is your sign to put yourself first for once!
