Foot Baths

A great way to relax after being on your feet all night is with a nice epsom salt foot bath. This requires little to no effort on your part, and is easily reusable! There is no need to go out and buy a fancy at-home spa kit, all you need is:

  • A plastic bin or tub (Here's a Link!)
  • Epsom salt
  • Hot water (the exact temperature is up to you)
  • Any essential oils or scents that would enhance your experience!
  • Optional: choose your favorite candle to add to the ambience!

First, take your tub and fill it with hot water. Next, add the epsom salt and any essential oils you deem necessary. Give that a stir so that the epsom salt is incorporated. Now, place your feet in the tub and relax! Watch a TV show, knit, play guitar - whatever your favorite calming activity is!
